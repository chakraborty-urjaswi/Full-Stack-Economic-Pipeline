# Full-Stack-Economic-Pipeline
# Global E-commerce sales of small luxuries- recession indicator - global hiring states- Indian hiring states pipeline


This is a purely academic venture meant to link buying trends to recession status and then to hiring states, essentially building a "Full-Stack Economic Pipeline". In the real world, investors and people are irrational. **The predictions or results of this project may not reflect real world events.**

Instead of jumping between disconnected proxy variables, a true full-stack economic model treats the economy as a chronological waterwheel where changes at the top (consumer spending) cascade directly into the center (macro hiring) and collect at the bottom (offshore enterprise delivery centers).

## Problem statement: 
***Can global e-commerce sales be used to predict recession, and does it impact global hiring states? Also, how do global hiring states and recession impact Indian IT health, mainly key players***

## Objective:
The project operates on a two-stage inference engine:
*   **Stage A:** E-commerce Sales Trends $\rightarrow$ Recession Probability
*   **Stage B:** Recession Probability $\rightarrow$ Global Hiring Demand
    *   Sub-point 1: Details about hiring sectors.
    *   Sub-point 2: Geographical variables.
Edge cases like pandemics and sudden geopolitical even are also considered for this. Finally, we also look beyond global hiring states, into the India context, looking at Indian IT health, espescially key top players.

The aim is to look at small luxury purchase and build a recession predictor, based on learning od the **lipstick index**, see how these recession indicators affect global hiring states and ultimately Predicting company-specific layoffs or hiring freezes for better insight in Corporate Risk Modeling.



## Expected learning in the course of this project:
 ***Multi-Source Data Integration:*** merging retail data, labor data, and macro-economic data.
 ***Handling Global Variance:*** Analyzing *Spatial-Temporal variance* Ex: does the "Buying Trend"   indicator work better in the US than in India or Europe?
 ***The Multi-Layered Prediction Pipeline:*** 
 **Macro-Tier:** E-commerce trends vs. GDP
    *   *The "Recession Indicator"*
*   **Industry-Tier:** Sector-specific health
    *   **Quick Commerce:** Evaluating growth and "boom" cycles.
    *   **Fashion Retail:** Monitoring slowing consumer demand.
*   **Company-Tier:** Individual corporate health signals
 ***Technical Strategy:*** Predict the probability of a "Layoff Event" occurring within the next 90 days. Use NLP (Natural Language Processing) to scan news headlines for keywords like "restructuring," "efficiency," "AI-native," or "cost-optimization."
***Creating the "Layoff & Freeze" Risk Score***

## Final expected outcome:
An interactive live dashboard that shows the relation between e-commerce sales and possible recession and shows the relation between that and global hiring states, and a probabilistic predictor for which companies are likely to increase hiring.
